    Public Integrity. Professional Methodology. Political Entrustment

    Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan was born on 19th April 1973 in Pattukkottai, Thanjavur district, Tamil Nadu. He was awarded MBBS degree from Sri Ramachandra Medical College & Research Institute, Chennai and received a Diploma in Diabetology from Raja Muthiah Medical College, Annamalai University. He is currently the District Secretary for DMK’s medical wing (Chennai West). He is the Founder, Vice Chairman & Correspondent of A.A.A Trust, A.R.J Group of Institutions and Sri Venkateswara group of institutions. The third generation Dravidian family from the Thanjavur district, Dr. Jeevagan follows the footsteps of his grand father, Sri. M. Krishnamoorthi, a legendary politician who was the 1st MLA of Peravurani Assembly Constituency in the year 1967. He served under the reign of Arignar Anna and for the DMK party for over 40 years. Dr. Jeevagan is the eldest son of Sri. K.M. Ayyanathan, who was the City Planning Committee Chairman, Chennai Corporation during the period 1997-2001. Sri KM Ayyanathan was an avid politician who served DMK for over 40 years. He was also the Thalaimai Seyal Kuzhu Urupinar and Podhu Kuzhu Urupinar for DMK.

    As an Academician, Dr. Jeevagan strongly believes that he has to play an important role in the upliftment of the society. The first thing that the academician must do is to address the issues in his inner society. He can do this in lectures, in publications, and mass media. He believes in research and propose solutions to the problems in his inner society. He expects academicians to stand for their ideals by criticizing negative phenomena and give guidelines towards society with solutions.

    Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan’s “Philosophy of Life”

    Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan’s “philosophy of life” means a mental framework for understanding how the world works and how you fit into this world. The philosophy of life would include things like how you decide what is “good” and “bad”, what “success” means, what your “purpose” in life is, how we should treat each other, etc. As an academician and Politician his understanding is that academician’s role is to educate people equally an the politician’s role is to uplift the equally educated people to reach great heights in this society which will strengthen our State of Tamil Nadu and our country.
