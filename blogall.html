    • A.R.J College student got 7th rank in state level

    A.R.J Post Graduate Engineering student Ms. Priyanka secures 7th Rank in the Anna University examination for the academic year 2019.College Correspondent Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan of A.R.J Group of Educational Institutions felicitated the student. Priyanka is a shining example to the standard of education in A.R.J Group of Educational Institutions.

    Mega Job Fair for A.R.J students

    Mega Job Fair conducted in the campus of A.R.J Group of Educational Institutions. 300 Engineering Graduates and 350 Arts and Science Graduates got placement in the campus selection in renowned Companies like Samsung, Wipro, Rikkan, Aptech, Fairplex, and Modin.

    Poor student’s paradise – A.R.J Group of Institutions

    Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan’s dream of quality education to Rural students coming true in Edayarnatham, Mannargudi. Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan’s Annai Ammani Ammal Trust offers world class Engineering Education to the students of Mannargudi at an affordable cost from 150 students to 2500 students in 18 Years. For a variety of Engineering courses in B.E and various post graduate courses, A.R.J. Engineering College is a favoured destination for all bright students.

    “Katral Nutpangal” book launch event of Sri Venketaswara Vidhyalaya

    Katral Nutpangal – a book launch event happened in Sri Venketaswara Vidhyalaya Auditorium. Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan launched the book and it was received by the head of Thanjavur Ancient city Arima Association. Mr.Jayapal, Pattukottai District Officer presided over the event.

    Venkateswara College Student got awarded “Ooviyasaaral Award”

    Venkateswara Arts and Science College students S. Santhini and R. Jyothika awarded “Ooviyasaaral Award” from Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan which was conducted by Namakal Kurinji Kabilar and District Tamil Sangam. Essay competition winner R. Neevi was awarded as “Elakiya Selvar Award”. Poetry competition winner P. Archana was awarded as “Paavalar Award”. Awards were felicitated by Correspondent of A.R.J Group of Institutions Dr. Jeevaga Ayyanathan.

    K.M. Ayyanathan Memorial Tribute

    A.R.J Engineering College and A.R.J Group of Institutions Officer K.M Ayyanathan’s Memorial Idol was launched by son Dr. Jeevagan Ayyanathan. Engineering students Head Loganathan, Polytechnic students Chief Kamalakannan, A.R.J College Managing Director Selvaraj and C.B.S.E School Deputy Chief Gopinathan, Vice Presidents, Professors, teachers and students were attended this event.
